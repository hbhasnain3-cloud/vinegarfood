About Us

How It All Began

Founded in 2005, Vinegar Food Restaurant started as a small family-owned establishment with a big dream: to bring authentic global flavors to our community.

Our founder, Chef Marco Vinegar, traveled the world for over a decade, learning traditional cooking techniques and collecting unique recipes. He envisioned a place where food lovers could experience these diverse culinary traditions without leaving town.

What started as a 20-seat eatery has now grown into an internationally recognized restaurant with locations across the globe, but our commitment to quality, authenticity, and innovation remains unchanged.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Sustainability

We source ingredients locally whenever possible and maintain ethical partnerships with suppliers who share our environmental values.

Passion

Every dish is prepared with genuine love for the culinary arts. We believe food should be an experience that delights all senses.

Community

We're committed to giving back to the communities we serve through local initiatives and supporting food education programs.
